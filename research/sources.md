# Sources — Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS

> **Topic chosen:** Cold outreach pipeline for B2B SaaS
> **Last updated:** 2026-06-11
> **Selection bar:** Every person here actively runs or has run outbound (as a rep, leader, or agency operator) and publishes tactical, first-hand material — not aggregated listicle content. Roles/links were checked in June 2026 but titles change fast in this space; re-verify before citing in the final playbook.

## How the 10 were chosen

A cold outreach *pipeline* has four layers. The list deliberately covers all of them instead of stacking 10 "cold email tips" voices:

1. **Messaging & copy** — what makes a cold email/call actually get a reply
2. **Cold calling & live conversations** — openers, objections, booking the meeting
3. **Systems & infrastructure** — list building, deliverability, Clay/automation, sequencing
4. **Multichannel & social** — LinkedIn touches, video prospecting, allbound
